2 · Semantic rules

Colour conveys function. Don't break the signal.
  • Green Save, positive secondary actions, success states, "Completed" status, on-target metrics.
  • Red Delete, destructive, errors, "Start breakdown" (this is a *negative event being captured*, hence red).
  • Orange Cancel, "in-flight" middling states, milestones 2 and 4 (response, start repair).
  • Blue Informational, links, "Active" status pill.
  • Gold #EDB012 Brand accent only. Never a whole-screen fill, never body text colour.
  • Dark #404149 Body text on light backgrounds; surface fill for dark admin view.
